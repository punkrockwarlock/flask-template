A Friend in Need

Get by with a little help from a friend

Who are we?

We aim to help communities help themselves.

We bring together the people who would like to help, with the people who need it. Sometimes we all need a bit of help, but we don't always know where to find it. This site helps to bring people together, so everyone has a place where they can look for help, or look for people to help.

How does it work?

You can offer to help anyone near you, help someone who is asking for it or ask for help when you need it.

Anything from helping someone mow their lawn, to helping an elderly neighbour do their shopping. All help is welcome.
